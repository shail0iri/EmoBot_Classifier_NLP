## For Sadness:
- Behavioral activation: Engage in small, achievable activities
- Opposite action: Do things that generate positive emotions
- Social connection: Reach out to one trusted person
- Self-compassion: Treat yourself with kindness, not criticism

## For Anxiety:
- Grounding technique: 5-4-3-2-1 method (5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste)
- Box breathing: Inhale 4s, hold 4s, exhale 4s, hold 4s
- Worry time: Schedule 15 minutes specifically for worrying

## For Anger:
- Time-out: Step away from the triggering situation
- Relaxation techniques: Progressive muscle relaxation
- Assertive communication: "I feel... when you... because..."

## For Stress:
- Time management: Break tasks into smaller chunks
- Self-care: Prioritize sleep, nutrition, exercise
- Mindfulness meditation: 5-10 minutes of present-moment awareness

## For Fear:
- Exposure hierarchy: Gradually face feared situations
- Reality testing: What is the actual probability?
- Coping statements: I have handled difficult things before

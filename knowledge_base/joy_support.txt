## Joy Support

Joy is worth noticing and protecting. Positive emotions can build resilience, connection, and motivation.

Savoring: Pause for 20 to 30 seconds and describe what feels good. Notice the body sensations, the people involved, and what made the moment meaningful.

Gratitude: Write down one specific thing that went well and why it mattered. Specific gratitude is more helpful than a general statement.

Sharing: If appropriate, tell someone you trust about the good moment. Sharing joy can strengthen relationships and make the emotion last longer.

Use joy as information. Ask, "What does this tell me about what I value or want more of in my life?"
